School History, Goals and Achievements

School Goals
Goals

As is best explained by its motto “Thamasoma Jyothir Gamaya”the goal of the school lies in helping the scores of children who enter the portals of the school every year to metamorphose into confident and enlightened individuals. It aims at creating an ambience that will foster a relationship of trust, love and understanding between the teaching fraternity and the students . The school aims at producing the youth of tomorrow by nurturing confidence and leadership qualities in them and helping them to emerge as peace and law abiding citizens committed to the development of the society and the nation at large. The other core values which the school emphasizes to ingrain in each student are empathy, righteousness, secularism, cooperation and acceptance which are in compliance with societal norms. The school with its emphasis on holistic education believes in following a highly efficacious policy in instilling self discipline and inculcating moral, secular and civic values.It also believes in infusing new ideas in the teaching methodology that are concurrent with the advances made in modern technology.

School History
History

Sishu Griha Montessori and High School which started as a fledgling institution in the year 1978 holds pride of place in Bangalore as being one of the few institutions that has effected a paradigm shift from teacher centred education to child centred education.

School Achivements
Achievements

To promote the emotional well being of the children which is the quintessence of the ethos of the school. To provide quality education through a judicious blend of tradition and modern technology –based learning that is within the parameters of our work culture. To stress on the all-round development of the child keeping in view that education is all encompassing and thereby bring out the academic, social, intellectual , cultural, emotional and spiritual development of the child. To broaden their realm of knowledge which will enable them to explore the world with confidence and help them to be a part of the global community. To enable them to grow in a child-centered milieu with the right weave of self-discipline and freedom. To bring about learning and self development through a spirit of enquiry.

Campus Life

school library

Was built in 1978

The library in Sishu Griha is always quiet as libraries are always quiet. We have many books on many topics such as comedy, fiction and even books that are based on making yourself a better person. We want to teach our kids about how imporant books are and the best part is that they enjoy them very much. We also want to make our children know that books take us to different worlds where your imagination is creating fantasies in the head. We all know that knowledge is important.

school auditorium

Was built in 1978

The school auditorium in Sishu Griha has been the room where the children have been most excited. It is, by far, the room in which everybody relaxes and enjoys. We watch our movies there and we also often watch educational movies. The children laugh and enjoy very much. The teachers put the people in the auditorium because it either is a school program or we want our children to rest, lay back and relax. Everybody cheers when the teacher says that it is finally our turn to visit the auditorium. This is our enjoyment place.

school science lab

Was built in 1978

Our science lab is very big. It is where our children learn about chemical operations to make something new. Science is now split in to three things: Chemistry, Physics and Biology. Biology is everything connected to living beings on planet Earth (our home planet among trillions of planets) Our science lab is very much like a classroom where we learn stuff about Chemistry and Biology. Physics is more like maths but still needs a laboratory for its own. The science lab is our learning hub.
